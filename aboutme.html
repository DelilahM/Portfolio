Asian Youth Services Committee (AYSC). The AYSC is somewhat similar to a community service organization. Within AYSC, I and the other members develop needed self-esteem and a sense of camaraderie and trust with other youth. The AYSC provides many activities and sponsors large events where we are taught to analyze, to organize, to write projections and budgets and to bring about a meaningful conclusion. We are taught how to delegate, to encourage others to participate and to have fun. We become skillful leaders.
